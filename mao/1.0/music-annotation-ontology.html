Music Annotation Ontology (MAO)

Vocabulary for the scholarly annotation of musical works.

The Music Annotation Ontology is described in detail in Lewis et al. (2023) with documentation available on the Beethoven in the House project website and in Hankinson et al. (2022).

Prefixes:
mao: <https://w3id.org/music-annotation/1.0#>
frbr: <http://purl.org/vocab/frbr/core#>
oa: <http://www.w3.org/ns/oa#>
rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>

Classes

MusicalIdeaClass
Subclass of: frbr:Work
Analogous to frbr:work, but on a local level, as an element of a larger piece. An abstract entity that refers to the musical thought behind a musical structure, especially a recurring one.
MusicalMaterialClass
Subclass of: frbr:Expression
The musical concept behind a musical Extract, independent of its various expressions in different arrangements or recordings. The realization of a MusicalIdea.
ExtractClass
Subclass of: frbr:Expression
A portion of music, independent of its manifestation in a particular edition, or its realization in any individual performance or media format.
SelectionClass
Subclass of: frbr:Manifestation
A collection of UUIDs that comprise a single musical object, at any level, within a single resource.
absenceSelectionClass
Subclass of: mao:Selection
A point marker that combines with an AbsenceQualification value (e.g., 'before') to indicate the location of an addition or abridgment.
